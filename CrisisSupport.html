Immediate Crisis Support for Students/Adults


At our organization, we understand that mental health crises can happen at any time. That's why we're here for you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to provide the support you need, when you need it most.

  • Call Our Helpline :
    Fell free to call us at the Emergency Contact Number (+91) 8708958504, we are available 24/7 just for you!

    Our text services at (0184) 2244 1569 are open 24/7, you can talk to medical professionals anonymously
  • Email Support :
    Our email services at abcemailUmatter@gmail.com are open throughout the day and night, with immediate responding
  • Call emergency responders :
    If you are experiencing uncontrollable anxiety, call at (0184) 2323 54243 you can reach out to our emergency responders who will come to your door to support you before you know it
